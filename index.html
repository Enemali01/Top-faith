Concepts & Objectives

The School is a non-denominational, co-educational, full boarding, governement-approved Secondary
School for the training and development of children in an atmosphere conducive for intellectual and moral
upbringing.

The School curriculum is derived from the provisions of the National Policy on Education. In addition to
WASSCE and NECO-SSCE, the School also incorporates the Cambridge O and A'levels, SAT and TOEFL
into its academic programs.
